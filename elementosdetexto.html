HIT4 is a Raspberry PI® boards implementing infrared transceivers – capable receive infrared signal from consumer’s remote controls, communicate it to the software for processing, accept control codes from the software and transmit infrared signals to consumer electronic appliances via externally attached emitters. Each device facilitates several independent transmission and reception channels. Every transmission channel has its own carrier frequency generator.
